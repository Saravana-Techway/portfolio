MMy First Attempt at Building a Diabetes Prediction Model

Welcome to my blog! Today, I’m excited to share my first experience with building a diabetes prediction model using machine learning. This project involved using a dataset to train a model that can predict whether a person has diabetes based on certain medical attributes.

For this project, I used the Pima Indians Diabetes Database, a popular dataset for machine learning tasks. The dataset contains various medical predictors such as glucose concentration, blood pressure, skin thickness, insulin levels, BMI, age, and more. The target variable indicates whether a person has diabetes (1) or not (0).
